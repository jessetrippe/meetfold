Privacy Policy

Last updated May 3, 2026

Fold does not collect, sell, rent, or share personal data.

Fold does not require an account. It does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking SDKs, or a Fold-operated backend service.

Your feeds, articles, read state, starred articles, filter rules, settings, and exports are stored locally on your device. When Fold refreshes feeds or fetches article content, it connects directly to the websites and feeds you choose to follow.

Exported files are created only when you request them and are shared using the standard iOS share sheet — nothing is sent anywhere automatically.

For privacy questions, contact jesset@me.com.
