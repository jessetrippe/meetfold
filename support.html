Support

Need help with Fold? Email jesset@me.com and include your device model, iOS version, and a short description of what happened.

Adding feeds

Paste either a website URL or a direct RSS/Atom feed URL. Fold will discover available feeds when it can.

Importing OPML

Export OPML from another RSS reader, then import it in Fold Settings.

Exporting your data

Use OPML for feeds or the full Fold backup export for feeds, articles, read state, starred state, and filter rules.

Backup & restore

Use the full Fold backup export in Settings to save your feeds, articles, read state, starred items, and filter rules. Import the file on any device to restore everything.
